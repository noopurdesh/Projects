RECIPE :  ZAIQEDAAR CHICKEN
*Serving Suggestion
Recipe:

  • Finely chop 5medium sized onions (400g) and saute in 6-7tbsp ghee (75g) in a pan for 10-12min till it turns golden brown.

  • Add 2tbsp (30g) ginger garlic paste with little water and saute for 2mins.

  • Prepare a paste of 7 medium sized tomatoes (500g) and add it to the pan and saute for 10-13mins till oil separates.

  • Add 3tbsp(25g) ITC Master Chef Zaiqedaar Chicken masala, optionally add ½ tsp ITC Master Chef chilli powder and stir fry for 2-3 minutes.

  • Add 4 cut green chillies, ½ inch finely chopped ginger, chopped coriander leaves for garnishing.

  • Adjust required consistency by adding water and add salt to taste.
